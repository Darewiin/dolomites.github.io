About This Project

The Dolomites Adventure Planner is a client-side travel dashboard that dynamically calculates trip costs, compares travel months, and generates personalized itineraries. Flight estimates include layover routing (BOS has no direct flights to VCE). All costs are sourced from 2026 airline and booking data.
